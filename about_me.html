About me

Hello, my name is Simeon. My journey into the world of programming began in high school, where I first encountered C#. This early exposure provided me with a solid logical foundation and sparked a passion that led me to the Technical University of Sofia, where I am currently majoring in "Information Systems in Industry." Throughout my university studies, I have gained experience with a wide range of languages, including Java, C, C++, and MySQL, but it was Python that truly impressed me with its elegance and vast possibilities. To transform this passion into professional expertise, I enrolled in the specialized Python track at SoftUni. It was during this intensive training that I built a comprehensive understanding of backend development, with a primary focus on the Django framework. I gained solid, hands-on knowledge of PostgreSQL, mastering how to integrate and manage relational databases directly within Django applications. To complement my backend skills, I also dived deep into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I pursued these frontend technologies not just to build interfaces, but to fully grasp the entire web ecosystem and understand how a server-side framework like Django powers a complete, seamless user experience. Today, I am a young, ambitious developer. I am constantly driven to learn new technologies and determined to apply my academic and practical skills to solve complex real-world problems.
